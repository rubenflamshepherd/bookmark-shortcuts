Bookmark Shortcuts is installed

Press Ctrl+1…4 on macOS (or Alt+1…4 on Windows/Linux) to open the first four items on your bookmarks bar.

One-time setup for slots 5–9

Chrome only auto-binds 4 keyboard shortcuts per extension. Slots 5–9 exist but are unbound. To enable them:

  1. Open chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
  2. Find Bookmark Shortcuts.
  3. Click each empty input for Open bookmark 5 through 9 and press the key combo you want (e.g. Ctrl+5…9 on macOS).

Chrome blocks regular links to chrome:// URLs, so the button above is what gets you there. If it doesn't work, copy chrome://extensions/shortcuts into the address bar.
