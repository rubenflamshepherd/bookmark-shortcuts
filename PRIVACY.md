# Privacy Policy

**Bookmark Shortcuts** does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

The extension reads your bookmarks bar and the contents of a selected bookmark folder locally in your browser solely to open the bookmark you triggered with a keyboard shortcut. No bookmark contents, URLs, browsing activity, or personal information are sent to the developer or any third party. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising, and no external network requests of any kind.

## Permissions used

- `bookmarks` — to read your bookmarks bar and the selected folder so the correct item can be opened.
- `favicon` — to display Chrome's locally cached site icons in the folder menu.
- `activeTab` — to temporarily show the folder menu in the active webpage after you press a shortcut.
- `scripting` — to inject that local menu into the active webpage; it does not run until you invoke a folder shortcut.
- `tabs` — to open the selected bookmark in a new tab or in the current tab, according to your preference.
- `storage` — to save that preference and briefly pass the selected folder to the popup.

All permissions are used entirely on-device. Nothing leaves your browser.
