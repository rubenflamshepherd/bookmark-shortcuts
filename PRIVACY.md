# Privacy Policy

**Bookmark Shortcuts** does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

The extension reads your bookmarks bar locally in your browser solely to open the bookmark you triggered with a keyboard shortcut. No bookmark contents, URLs, browsing activity, or personal information are sent to the developer or any third party. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising, and no external network requests of any kind.

## Permissions used

- `bookmarks` — to read the items on your bookmarks bar so the correct one can be opened when you press a shortcut.
- `tabs` — to open the selected bookmark in a new tab.

Both permissions are used entirely on-device. Nothing leaves your browser.
